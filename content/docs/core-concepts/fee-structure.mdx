---
title: Fee Structure
description: Trading, funding, and liquidation fees on FluxPerp.
---

FluxPerp charges fees in USDC. Fees are deducted from the user's margin account
when trades settle.

| Fee type | Typical value |
|---|---|
| Maker fee | 0.01% |
| Taker fee | 0.02% |
| Liquidation fee | 1.00% |
| Funding | Variable |

## Maker and taker

Maker orders add resting liquidity to the book. Taker orders remove liquidity by
matching immediately against resting orders.

## Liquidation

Liquidation fees compensate liquidators and contribute to insurance reserves.
See [Liquidation](/docs/core-concepts/liquidation) for mechanics.
