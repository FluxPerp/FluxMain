---
title: Funding Rate
description: Funding payments for FluxPerp perpetual markets.
---

Funding is a periodic payment exchanged between long and short traders. It
helps keep perpetual prices close to the index price.

## Direction

| Market condition | Typical payment |
|---|---|
| Perp above index | Longs pay shorts |
| Perp below index | Shorts pay longs |

## Timing

FluxPerp snapshots funding every 8 hours. Funding changes margin balance, not
position size.

## Where to see it

The active market top bar displays current funding and time until the next
snapshot.
